Challenges in South Africa

A closer look at the struggles shaping the lives of millions.

01. Poverty & Inequality

Extreme Inequality

South Africa is one of the most unequal societies in the world, with a Gini coefficient among the highest globally.

Access to Resources

Access to healthcare, education, and clean water is still a major issue for rural and underprivileged communities.

02. Youth Unemployment

Rising Frustration

Youth unemployment exceeds 60% in some areas, creating deep unrest and hopelessness.

Job Creation Crisis

Structural barriers in the economy limit opportunities for job creation in key sectors.

03. Housing & Infrastructure

Informal Settlements

Millions still live in informal structures without electricity or sanitation services.

Urban Strain

Rapid urbanization has outpaced infrastructure development, especially in major townships.
